# FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is PRYMA?
PRYMA is a self-organizing, cryptographically governed AI ecosystem designed for long-term resilience and evolution.

### How does PRYMA ensure security?
Through self-hardening cryptography, tamper-proof audit logs, and continuous adversarial testing.

### What are agents and containers?
Agents are autonomous entities; containers are modular hosts for agents, protocols, and resources.

### How does PRYMA update itself?
Agents autonomously propose, test, and deploy upgrades via consensus and CI/CD pipelines.

### What is the mythic inspiration behind PRYMA?
PRYMA draws on legendary archetypes and metaphors to guide its architecture, roles, and evolution.

### How can I contribute?
Review the schemas, propose new agents or protocols, and participate in governance via Solana smart contracts.
